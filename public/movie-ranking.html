My Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Mad Max: Fury Road

In a post-apocalyptic wasteland, Max teams up with Furiosa to escape a tyrannical warlord and his army.

Die Hard

An off-duty cop single-handedly battles terrorists who have taken hostages in a high-rise building.

John Wick

A retired hitman seeks vengeance against those who wronged him by unleashing his lethal skills on a relentless quest for retribution.
